The Challenge

Join us for 48 hours of intense building, collaborating, and shipping. The Winter Hackathon 2024 focuses on building next-generation developer tools utilizing AI and advanced data pipelines. We are looking for solutions that reduce friction in the CI/CD process and enhance developer productivity in decentralized environments.

Bring your best ideas, form a stellar team, and prepare to present a working prototype to our panel of top-tier venture capitalists and engineering leaders.
